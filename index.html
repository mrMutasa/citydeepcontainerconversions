Quality, durable, tailor made container solutions.

City Deep Container Conversions is a container conversion company which specializes in all aspects of container solutions. We provide turnkey solutions all throughout South Africa and beyond, our services range from the hiring, selling and the custom conversions of any grade of containers. We are however not limited to the conversions of containers but we also offer a wide range of other services which include construction of gates, balustrades and wooden staircases. .

All our containers adhere to international standards of quality, size and security. As a standard, our containers are by default, waterproof, highly durable and are sustainable for any type of climate.
